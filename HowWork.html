How OpenSpace Works

Finding a Parking Space

Imagine cruising into the city stress-free, knowing you'll easily find a parking spot. OpenSpace provides real-time updates on available parking spaces near you. No more circling the blocks – just a seamless parking experience.

Dynamic Tariffs and Zones

Our app introduces dynamic tariffs based on your distance from the city center. Closer parking might cost a bit more, but you'll have the flexibility to make sustainable choices. Park further away for cost savings and reduced CO2 emissions.

Accurate Navigation

OpenSpace provides precise navigation to the nearest parking garages with available spaces. Save time and fuel with detailed directions, ensuring you reach your destination efficiently.

Sustainable Options

We're committed to promoting eco-friendly mobility. Get information on public transportation, bike paths, and walking routes. Make choices that contribute to a greener future.

Easy Reservation and Payment

Reserve your parking spot hassle-free with OpenSpace. Upon arrival, our license plate reader recognizes your vehicle, and the payment process begins automatically. Easy, secure, and efficient.

Supporting Accessibility

OpenSpace addresses the needs of all users. Choose reserved spots for disabled parking or charging stations for electric cars. Pay a little extra for the assurance of these dedicated spaces.
